Column 2(About BDU)

Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Digital University, Bangladesh is the first specialized government digital university in Bangladesh. The Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh enacted a law in the National Assembly on July 26, 2016 to establish this university. .
